Middle Room Training

Learn how to safely and responsibly use the Middle Room before booking.

1. Safety Guidelines

  • Always keep volume at a safe level to protect your hearing.
  • Report any damaged equipment to a teacher immediately.
  • Do not unplug or rewire equipment unless instructed.

2. Equipment Usage

  • Use headphones when practicing unless told otherwise.
  • Handle instruments and cables with care.
  • Turn off all equipment after use.

3. Room Expectations

  • Arrive on time for your booking.
  • Leave the room clean and organised.
  • Respect other students’ bookings and time slots.
